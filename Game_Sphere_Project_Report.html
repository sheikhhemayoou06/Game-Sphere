1. Executive Summary

Game Sphere is India's comprehensive National Sports Digital Infrastructure platform, designed to digitise and modernise sports management across the country — from national federations to district-level grassroots sports.

The platform provides an end-to-end solution covering tournament management, player registration with Unique Sports Identifiers (USI), real-time match scoring, financial tracking, team management, venue booking, grievance resolution, and much more.

Built across 7 development phases, the MVP now contains 36 distinct pages powered by a robust NestJS backend with 13 modules and 18 Prisma database models.
